What is EDU-thon ?

EDU-thon is the collaboration of programmers, designers and inventor to code, design and build innovative solutions that tackle real world problems caused in education sector due to corona pandemic.

Its a weekend of geeking at its finest for improving the industries that affect all that live or work in a house or building.It has quickly become a global community of innovators that include all elements of the build environment.

This year marks the 1st edition of Eduthon,IIIT Lucknow. The Hackathon shall take place from 28th to 30th August ,2020 Virtually.

The first edition of Eduthon promises to be the grandest, greatest and the most magnificent beginning that a fest could possible have.

Why Education ?

Education is what survives when what has been learned has been forgotten.

India came under the wave of corona pandemic in march followed by a lockdown. Everyone was talking about how this will impact the industries,jobs etc. but an important topic like Education skipped everyone's minds.

Colleges, Schools, Tutions and Coaching centres have been stuck with issues they were not prpared for , multiple issues are being faced , conduction of classes , evaluation etc.

TO tackle the innumerous issues that cross our minds on a daily basis we have chosen education to be the theme of the hackathon.

What is the team size ?
The team size can range from 2 to 4 members.
Who can attend ?
Beginners, pros, literally everyone. Anyone with a passion for learning, or primarily, undergraduate and highschool programmers are welcome to attend. Even if you cant code, we can teach you or you can help out in the hackathon.
How can I help ?
We appreciate all who want to support our cause and we definitely need your awesome skills. Even if someone cant code, volunteers can support the organizing team during the hackathon with various tasks. Its always good to have a helping hand.
What can I build ?
Possibly something that resonates with the theme of the event, that is social good. It can be a website, software, an applet or maybe even a piece of hardware. Wed love to see anything that solves a social problem or goes with our agenda.
What if I dont have a team or an idea ?
Even if you dont have a team or an idea, that's totally fine! We'll have a team forming event right before development starts. Well also have an ideation event where everyone will be brainstorming to generate new ideas.
